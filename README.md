# CloudSnap

A dual-mode lightweight camera that lets you snap freely without storage worries.

Tired of phone storage filled with receipts, parking spot photos, or random shots from kids? Frustrated with spending hours deleting photos after traveling? CloudSnap’s Moment Mode and Star Mode bring you a whole new photography experience.

## Core Features

### Moment Mode - Snap and Forget, Auto-Fade
Need to temporarily record receipts, parking spots, or meeting whiteboards? Take quick shots of road signs or menus while traveling? Turn on Moment Mode, and photos will be retained for a limited time before auto-deleting—no more manual cleanup.

### Star Mode - One-Tap Save, Forever Preserve
Want to keep stunning travel views, your child’s cute moments, or warm gatherings with friends? Switch to Star Mode to lock in those heartfelt seconds and build a personalized memory album.

## Applicable Scenarios
- Snap freely during travels, no need to organize on the go—unwanted photos fade automatically
- Kids can snap as much as they want, with auto-cleanup to save storage
- Perfect for temporary daily records: receipts, parking spots, check-ins—use and forget without taking up space
- A must-have for minimalists, keeping your photo album neat and organized
